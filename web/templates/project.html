Implemented evaluation methods used in this project.

1. Uncertainty methods
  • Confidence baseline (MSP): single-pass softmax confidence (`1 - MSP` as uncertainty). Used as a crude baseline only.
  • MC Dropout: multiple stochastic forward passes with dropout active at inference; mean prediction + uncertainty from disagreement/entropy.
  • Deep Ensemble: aggregate predictions from multiple separately trained checkpoints; used for stronger uncertainty/OOD behavior.
